7. change these sentences into negative and interrogative!


Negative sentence pada dasarnya kalimat yang menggunakan kata "tidak" atau "not", dan interrogative adalah kata tanya.

Dilihat dari kalimat-kalimatnya, ada 2 tenses yang dipakai, present dan past.

Formulasi untuk simple present:

(-) S + do/does not + Verb 1 + O

(?) Do/does + S + Verb 1 + O ?

Formulasi untuk simple past:

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O ?


Pembahasan

1. The tanker didn't carry liquid cargoes.

  Did the tanker carry liquid cargoes?

2. We don't use these tools everyday. They aren't of very high quality!

   Do we use these tools everyday? Are they of very high quality?

3. The engineer doesn't overhaul the bilge pump once a month and then doesn't always make a report.

  Does the engineer overhaul the bilge pump once a month and then always make a report?

4. I don't try to meet the Chief Engineer.

  Do I try to meet the Chief Engineer?

5. She doesn't always wake up early.

  Does she always wake up early?

6. He didn't go to his office this morning.

  Did he go to his office this morning?

7. Someone didn't paint the bulkhead.

  Did someone paint the bulkhead?

8. Yesterday he didn't visit the cinema with his friends.

   Did he visit the cinema with his friends yesterday?

9. MV Karrier doesn't leave the port at 1700 hours.

   Does MV Karrier leave the port at 1700 hours?

10. I can't speak English.

    Can I speak English?

25. Change these sentences into Indirect speech!​


Jawaban:Linda asked where his sister was.Bayu said that he didn't do the homework.Ida said that she had seen him.Gilang said that he was tired.Zee said that he went to karaoke Suka-suka

Penjelasan:

Indirect speech, also known as indirect discourse, is a way of reporting someone's words or thoughts without quoting them directly. Instead of using quotation marks to enclose the speaker's exact words, indirect speech uses words like "said," "told," or "asked" to introduce the spoken words and may change the tense of the original statement. For example, instead of saying "She said, 'I am going to the store'," indirect speech would say "She said that she was going to the store."

Passive: Magazine is being read by my father now.

Passive: Gado-gado was eaten by them yesterday.

4. The students were buying fried rice.

Passive: Fried rice was being bought by the students.

Passive: A hole is going to be dug by Mr Jaja.

Penjelasan:

1. Passive simple present tense:

Object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject

2. Passive present continuous tense:

Object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by subject

3. Passive simple past tense:

Object + was/were + verb 3 + by subject

4. Passive past continuous tense:

Object + was/were + being + verb 3 + by subject

5. Passive simple future tense with be going to:

Object + am/is/are + going to be + verb 3 + by subject
