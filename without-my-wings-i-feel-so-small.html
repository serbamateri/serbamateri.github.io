said = berkata/menyampaikan

“said” merupakan kata kerja (verb) bentuk ke dua (verb 2) dari kata dasarnya yakni “say”. Kalimat tersebut merupakan kalimat Simple Past Tense.

Jadi apa itu kalimat Simple Past Tense? Kalimat Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan kejadian yang sudah terjadi dimasa lalu atau lampau dan kejadian tersebut sudah selesai terjadi atau berakhir.

 

Formula untuk kalimat Simple Past Tense adalah:

Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Compliment

Subject + to be (was/were) + Object/Compliment

Keterangan: Simple present tense [SPT] dan Simple present continuous [SPC]


Pembahasan

Simple present tense

Positive form

Subject + V1 (-s, -es, -ies)

He goes to schoolMy daughter lives in South AfricaIt snows a lot during the winter

Subject: (He, She, It)


Negative form

Subject + does/do NOT + V1

My mother doesn’t speak good englishIt doesn’t rain in the summerShe doesn’t study Math on Monday

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)


Question form

Does/Do + Subject + V1

Does he go to school?Does she live in Paris?Do you like apple?

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)


Present Continuous

Positive form

(+) Subject + to be + V-ing

He is sleepingI am readingThey are swimming

Negative form

(-) Subject + to be + not + V-ing

He is not sleepingI am not readingThey are not swimming

Question form

(?) To be + subject + V-ing?

Is she sleeping?Are you reading?Are they swimming?
