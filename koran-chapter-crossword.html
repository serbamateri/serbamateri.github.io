Across:

1. Related to the disease, injuries, an treatment of animals : Veterinary

2. Caring taken to avoid danger or mistakes : Caution

3. The amount of medicine prescribed by physicians : Dosage

4. Expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound : Cough

Down:

1. None : Zero

2. Taking special care of someone or something : Attention

3. Drug : Medicine

4. A soft, silver - white chemical element that is found in salt : Sodium

5. Not natural : Artificial

6. A sign of illness: Symptom

Pembahasan

1. Related to the disease, injuries, an treatment of animals : Veterinary

Berhubungan dengan penyakit, luka, penanganan binatang : penyakit hewan

2. Caring taken to avoid danger or mistakes : Caution

Penanganan yang diambil untuk menhindari bahaya atau kesalahan: peringatan

3. The amount of medicine prescribed by physicians : Dosage

Jumlah obat yang diresepkan oleh perawat  : Dosis

4. Expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound : Cough

Mengeluarkan udara dari paru-paru dengan suara keras tiba-tiba : batuk

1. None : Zero

2. Taking special care of someone or something : Attention

Menjaga seseorang atau sesuatu : perhatian

3. Drug : Medicine

Obat : obat

Lembut, element kimia berwarna putih silver yang ditemukan pada garam : sodium

5. Not natural : Artificial

6. A sign of illness: Symptom
