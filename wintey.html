6. Apa deskripsi winter dalam bahasa inggris


Winter is the coldest season of the year in polar and temperate zones (winter does not occur in most of the tropical zone). It occurs after autumn and before spring in each year. Winter is caused by the axis of the Earth in that hemisphere being oriented away from the sun. Different cultures define different dates as the start of winter, and some use a definition based on weather.

10. Q. 50+ (easy)winter memiliki 20 album, winter membagikan 15 album nya kepada giselle. lalu winter membeli lagi 5 album berapa album yang di miliki winter sekarang...? __ Rules: Teliti!Pke cara!No ngasal!No kalku!Budidayakan membaca!Lengkap >> BA__ ​


Jawaban:

20 - 15 + 5

20 - 15 + 5

5 + 5

10

Jadi album winter sisa 10

Banyak album winter sekarang adalah 10 album

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui :

banyak album winter = 20diberikan ke Giselle = 15dibeli lagi = 5

Ditanya :

banyak album winter sekarang?

Dijawab :

Album sekarang = banyak album - diberikan ke Giselle + album yang dibeli lagi

Album sekarang = 20 album - 15 album + 5 album

Album sekarang = (20 - 15 + 5) album

Album sekarang = (5 + 5) album

Album sekarang = 10 album

Kesimpulan

Jadi, banyak album sekarang adalah 10 album
