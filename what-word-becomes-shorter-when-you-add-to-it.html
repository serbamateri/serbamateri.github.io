36. What number when decreased by 15% becomes 102?​


Jawab:

Kita asumsikan angka yang ingin kita ketahui adalah X . Angka ini merupakan nilai awal yang belum diturunkan 15%. Maka dapat kita buat persamaannya sebagai berikut:

X = 102 + Y  .... (1)

[tex]\frac{15}{100} . X= Y[/tex] ..... (2)

Substitusikan persamaan 1 kedalam persamaan 2,

[tex]\frac{15}{100} . (102+Y)= Y\\15(102+Y)=100Y\\1530+15Y=100Y\\85Y=1530\\Y=18\\[/tex]

Didapat nilai Y=18, maka nilai X dapat diketahui sebagai berikut:

X= 102+ 18

X=120

Oleh karena itu angka yang jika diturunkan sebesar 15% nilainya 102 adalah X=120

38. the length of iron can be...... when it is cooled.(shorter/longer)​


Jawaban:

Cast iron is a group of iron-carbon alloys with a carbon content more than 2%.[1] Its usefulness derives from its relatively low melting temperature. The alloy constituents affect its colour when fractured: white cast iron has carbide impurities which allow cracks to pass straight through, grey cast iron has graphite flakes which deflect a passing crack and initiate countless new cracks as the material breaks, and ductile cast iron has spherical graphite "nodules" which stop the crack from further progressing.

Various examples of cast iron

Carbon (C) ranging from 1.8 to 4 wt%, and silicon (Si) 1–3 wt%, are the main alloying elements of cast iron. Iron alloys with lower carbon content are known as steel.

Cast iron tends to be brittle, except for malleable cast irons. With its relatively low melting point, good fluidity, castability, excellent machinability, resistance to deformation and wear resistance, cast irons have become an engineering material with a wide range of applications and are used in pipes, machines and automotive industry parts, such as cylinder heads, cylinder blocks and gearbox cases. It is resistant to damage by oxidation.
