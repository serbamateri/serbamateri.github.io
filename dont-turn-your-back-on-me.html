8. 15. "Then, just move on to the rest of your material." The underlined(Move On) word is similar in meaning with .... a. turn back b. begin again C. go ahead d. hang on


Jawaban:

C. go ahead

Penjelasan:

"Move On" means to continue to the next thing or activity after completing or dealing with something. "Go ahead" also means to continue or proceed with something, especially after receiving permission or encouragement. In this context, the speaker is advising the listener to move on to the remaining material after finishing one part. Therefore, "go ahead" is the most similar phrase in meaning to "move on

C. Go ahead
The answer is go ahead
