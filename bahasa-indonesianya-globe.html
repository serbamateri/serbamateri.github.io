5. globe dalam bahasa inggris yg berarti


Globe adalah bola dunia berukuran kecil dalam bentuk tiga dimensi dengan kemiringan 66 ⅛ ° pada garis ekliptika (bidang edar bumi)dan dengan kemiringan 23 ⅛ ° dari matahari. 9. apa yg dimaksud globe gantung ,globe bertiang, globe beralas


Globe adalah gambaran bumi yang diperkecil dengan bentuk yang hampir mendekati keadaan bentuk bumi. Globe berbentuk bola yang dapat berputar dan menggambarkan bola bumi, bola langit, bulan, atau suatu planet di ruang angkasa.

Globe dapat dimanfaatkan untuk kepentingan politik, yaitu globe yang memuat batas-batas negara dan kota-kota besar dari negara-negara di dunia.
Globe dapat digunakan sebagai alat peraga di dunia pendidikan untuk mempermudah pemahaman tentang bumi dan planet di ruang angkasa.
Globe juga digunakan sebagai sumber ilmu pengetahuan karena globe dapat menggambarkan iklim, vegetasi, arus laut, dan unsur-unsur geografis lainnya.

Globe dapat dibedakan berdasarkan cara globe diletakkan, yaitu globe bertiang, globe gantung, dan globe beralas. Dari ketiga jenis globe ini yang banyak digunakan adalah globe bertiang.

14. deskripsi penghapus,penggaris,peta,globe,pintu,jendela dalam bahasa inggris


Penghapus=eraser, penggaris=ruler, peta=map, globe=globe, pintu=door, jendela= Window

Maaf klo salah ya

DESCRIPTION OF :

1) ERASER (penghapus)

It is one of the stationery equipment, which is a soft rubber, that able to remove marks made by pencils.

2) RULER (penggaris)

It is a measuring device and drawing tools for drawing straight lines.

There are various types of rulers, from the straight to the triangular (usually the right footed triangle and right triangle 30 ° -60 °). Ruler can be made of plastic, metal, ribbon-shaped and so on, some can even be folded.

3) MAP (peta)

It is a picture of the entire or part of the earth's surface in a flat plane, which is minimized using a certain scale. The study of maps is called cartography, while people who work as mapmakers are called cartographs.

4) GLOBE (bola dunia)

It is an imitation of an earth ball that is minimized and formed to resemble the original shape of the earth. Unlike ordinary maps depicted on a flat plane (usually paper), the globe is depicted on a ball, so that its shape resembles the real earth.

5) DOOR (pintu)

It is a connecting device that made by punching the wall and covering it again with a board that can be opened and closed. The function of the door is as a separator between the area inside the building and the outside of the building, and as a separator between each room in the same building.

6) WINDOW (jendela)

It is a hole that can be closed. Windows are installed on the walls of buildings and cars, which function as an outlet for air and light. Windows usually only have a square and round shape. But as the time evolved, it also affects the shape of architecture, so now windows has many different shapes.
