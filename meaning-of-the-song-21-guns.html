5. What is the meaning of the song​


Jawab

Songs are compositions of tone or sound in the order, combination, and temporal relationships (usually accompanied by musical instruments) to produce musical compositions that have unity and continuity (containing rhythm). And the range of rhythmic notes or sounds is also called a song.

Songs can be sung solo, together (duet), threes (trio) or in rollicking (koir). The words in the song are usually in rhythmic poetry, but some are religious or free prose. Songs can be categorized in many types, depending on the size used.

25. A book have 21 grama. determine the meaning of 21!b. determine the meaning of gram !tolong bantuannya yaaa!


Jika kata "determine" dalam perintah dalam pertanyaan A dan B adalah untuk menjelaskan atau mendefinisikan sebuah kata dalam kalimat "A book have 21 gram" maka :

A. The meaning of "21" in the sentence above means that the book has 21 gram in weight. It shows the amount of weight of a stuff which is a book in this case.

(Maksud dari 21 dalam kalimat di atas berarti bahwa buku tersebut memiliki berat 21 gram. Itu (21 gram) menunjukkan berat suatu benda yang mana benda itu adalah sebuah buku dalam kalimat ini)

B. The meaning of "gram" is a metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram.

(satuan metrik massa sama dengan seperseribu kilogram)

*sekalian koreksi : di dalam kalimat terdapat subjek yaitu "book" dengan artikel "a" yang berarti subjek itu adalah singular atau tunggal. Maka kata kerjanya seharusnya "has" bukan "have" .
