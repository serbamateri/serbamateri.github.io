7. Cara membuat jus mangga dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Ingredients:
- 2 ripe mangoes
- 1 cup of water
- 2 tablespoons of sugar
 - a few ice cubes

makes 4 servings

steps:
1. First, wash the mangoes to remove it from any dirt and then peel the ripe mangoes.
2. Second, cut the peeled mangoes into small pieces.
3. Thirdly, put them in a blender together with the sugar, water and crushed ice cubes.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Then, run the blended mixture through a sieve.
6. Throw away the leftover pulp and mango fibres.
7. Lastly, Serve the juice into glasses.

bahan yang diperlukan:
- 2 mangga matang
- secangkir air putih
- 2 sendok makan gula
- beberapa es batu

membuat 4 gelas

langkah:
1. pertama, mencuci dulu mangganya supaya bersih dari kotoran lalu kemudian mengupasnya
2. kedua, potonglah mangga yg sudah terkupas menjadi kecil
3. ketiga, masukan potongan mangga, ke dalam blender bersama gula, air dan es batu yg sudah dihancurkan
4. campurkan sampai halus
5. Lalu, tuanglah campuran mangga itu melalu saringan
6. buanglah serat dan mangga yg tersisa
7. Terahkir, sajikan jus ke dalam gelas

How to Make Melon Juice

Ingredients:
- a half of melon
- 1 tsp of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- ice cubes (optional)

Steps:
1. First, cut the melon fruit into small parts to make it smooth faster.
2. Next, put all the melon parts into the blender with sugar and salt.
3. After that, add some ice cubes to make it cooler.
4. Then, blend it until smooth.
5. Finally, pour the melon juice into a glass.
6. Ta-da! A fresh, delicious melon juice is ready to be served~

How to Make Mango Juice

Mango is common fruit in our country. There are plenty types of it. And when it comes to the 'mango season', you can enjoy the taste of mango via mango juice as the alternative. Here is a simple recipe of mango juice.

What you need

Blender

A Mango

Ice cubes

1 cup of water

2 tablespoons of sugar

A Sieve

Steps

Wash the mango and then peel it.

Cut it into small pieces.

Put them in a blender with ice cubes, two tbs sugar, and one cup of water.

Blend it.

When it is smooth enough, strain the juice using a sieve before pour it into a glass.

Now you mango juice is ready to drink

How to Make Mango Juice

Ingredients :

• 1 cup chopped ripe manggo

• 1/2 cup low-fat milk

• 1/2 cup ice

• 1/4 cup plain low-fat yogurt

• 1 tablespoon of honey

Steps :

1. Put the mango, milk, ice, yogurt and honey in a blender.

2. Blend until smooth and frothy.

3. Pour the smoothies into the glass and it's ready to drink.

Ingredients

1 cup (165 g) peeled and chopped red or pink guavas

1 teaspoon (4 g) sugar

1/2 cup (120 ml) cold water

Ice cubes


1. Peel and chop the guavas. Wash several red or pink guavas. Use a vegetable peeler to remove the skin. While you can leave the peel on, it will make the juice thicker and grittier. Chop the guavas into chunks. You should end up with 1 cup (165 g).

2. Measure the ingredients into a blender. Put the chopped guava into a blender or food processor. Add 1 teaspoon (4 g) of sugar and 1/2 cup (120 ml) of cold water. Put the lid on the blender or processor.


3. Blend the guava mixture. Blitz the guava mixture just until the guava is smooth and mixed with the water. Avoid blending the mixture for too long or the seeds will become finely ground. This will make it difficult to strain them out and your guava juice will be gritty.


4. Strain the guava juice. Set a fine mesh strainer over a bowl. For even smoother juice, line the strainer with cheesecloth. Scoop the guava mixture into the strainer and use a spoon to gently push the mixture through it.


5. Add ice cubes and serve the guava juice.Place several ice cubes into 2 small serving glasses. Pour the strained guava juice into the glasses and serve it immediately.
