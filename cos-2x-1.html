11. buktikan cos^4x(1-tan^2x)=cos^2x-sin^2x


[tex]\cos^4 x(1 -\tan^2 x) = \cos^4 x \left( 1- \dfrac{\sin^2 x}{\cos^2 x} \right)\\= \cos^4 x - \cos^4 x \cdot \dfrac{\sin^2 x}{\cos^2 x}\\= \cos^4 x - \cos^2 x \sin^2 x\\= \cos^2 x (\cos^2 x -\sin^2 x)[/tex]

Sepertinya pernyataan [tex]\cos^4 x(1-\tan^2 x)=\cos^2 x-\sin^2 x[/tex] salah. Kita buktikan dengan contoh penyangkal.

Ambil [tex]x=30^0[/tex]

[tex]\cos^4 30^0 (1-\tan^2 30^0) = \left(\dfrac{1}{2} \sqrt{3} \right)^4 \left(1 -\left( \dfrac{1}{3} \sqrt{3} \right)^2 \right)\\= \left(\dfrac{9}{16} \right) \left(1 -\dfrac{1}{3} \right)\\= \left(\dfrac{9}{16} \right) \left(\dfrac{2}{3} \right)\\=\dfrac{3}{8}[/tex]

dan

[tex]\cos^2 x-\sin^2 x =\cos^2 30-\sin^2 30\\=\left( \dfrac{1}{2} \sqrt{3} \right)^2 - \left( \dfrac{1}{2} \right)^2\\=\dfrac{3}{4}-\dfrac{1}{4}\\=\dfrac{2}{4}\\=\dfrac{1}{2}[/tex]

Jadi, pernytaan tersebut tidak berlaku
