29. "when we got to the stadium, we found that they had already started the game" ubah ke passive voice, tlg bantuu yaa


Subject : English

Category : Passive Voice

Sub-category : Passive in the present simple tense

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

--------

Passive voice adalah kalimat pasif. Kalimat pasif adalah kalimat di mana subjectnya adalah yang menerima aksi, bukan yang melakukan aksi.

Konstruksi dasar passive voice :  S + be + past participle (v₃) .

-----------

Soal :

When we got to the stadium, we found that they had already started the game.

Bagian yang ditebalkan adalah klausa yang dapat dijadikan pasif karena ada object.

Klausa tersebut menggunakan past perfect (S + had + past participle).

Struktur kalimat pasif dalam past perfect adalah : S + had + been + past participle.

-------------

Jawaban :

When we got to the stadium, we found that the game had already been started (by them).

-----------------------------
