Jawaban:

Degree Of Comparison

(positif= comparative-superlative)

great= greater- the greatestlazy= lazier- the laziestinteresting= more interesting- the most interestingdangerous= more dangerous- the most dangerousboring= more boring- the most boring

Penjelasan:

Comparative adalah membandingan antara dua hal. Yang artinya 'lebih'

Superlative adalah perbandingan yang menunjukkan tingkat perbandingan yang 'paling' atau 'ter'.

Rumus Comparative:

subjek + to be + adjective + -er (+ than( subjek))S + Verb + Adverb + -er (+ than + S + V)S + to be + more + adjective + (+ than + S)

Rumus Superlative:

S + to be + the (adjective) + -est (+ objek)S + V + Adverb + -est (+ objek)S + to be + the most + adjective (+ objek)S + V + most + adverb (+ objek)Semoga bisa membantu:)Maaf jika ada yang salah
