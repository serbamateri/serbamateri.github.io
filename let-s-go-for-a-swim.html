34. (conditional sentence) If the weather were not bad, I could go for a swim -the fact of the sentence is?


I can swim if the weather is not bad

Maaf apabila jawabannya salah

If the weather were not bad, I could go for a swim

-the fact of the sentence is?

Penjelasan :

Pernyataan if clause di atas termasuk bentuk Conditional sentence type 2.

Conditional sentence type 2 Pengandaian yang belum terwujud karena kondisi tertentu pada saat ini ataupun nanti-nantiBerlawanan dengan kenyataan yang ada.Jika If clause bentuknya kalimat negatif , maka faktanya berbanding terbalik ( pernyataan positif  dalam bentuk kalimat simple present/future)Jika if clause bentuknya kalimat positif , maka faktanya kebalikannya ( pernyataan negatif dalam bentuk kalimat simple present/future )Contoh :If i were you, i would go to London.The fact : Iam not you, so i can not go to London.

Jawaban :

If the weather were not bad, i could go for swim.

The fact is the weather is bad , so i can not go for swim.
