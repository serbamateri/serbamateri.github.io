1. Imagery: The song uses vivid and descriptive language to paint a picture and evoke emotions. It captures the essence of a golden hour, which is the period of warm and soft light during sunrise or sunset.

2. Metaphorical Language: The song may use metaphors to convey deeper meanings or emotions. For example, "golden hour" might symbolize a moment of happiness or a special time in someone's life.

3. Poetic Language: The lyrics of the song may employ poetic devices such as rhymes, alliteration, or repetition to create a melodic and rhythmic flow.

4. Figurative Language: The song might use figurative language, such as similes or personification, to add depth and imagery. It helps to create a sense of connection and resonance with the listeners.

5. Emotional Expression: The song likely expresses emotions, whether it's joy, nostalgia, reflection, or a combination of different feelings. The lyrics and vocal delivery contribute to conveying these emotions to the listeners.

Overall, "Golden Hour" likely showcases a blend of descriptive, metaphorical, and poetic language to create a meaningful and emotionally resonant song.

13. What tense used in the lyrics ? Mentions it !Song: Goodbye (air supply)​


Jawaban/Answer:

Present tense atau simple present tense(kalau mau lebih lengkap, disarankan yang ini)

Explanation/Penjelasan:

Bukti lagu ini menggunakan present tense bisa dilihat dari kata kerja(verb) di semua baris.  Seperti baris dua "And I know how hard you try", kata "know"( atau knows buat kata ganti orang ketiga) disitu adalah kata kerja(verb) simple present tense. Simple past tense dari kata 'know' itu adalah 'knew'. Satu contoh lagi itu baris tiga "You deserve to have so much more". Kata kerja(verb) disitu adalah 'deserve'(atau deserves buat kata ganti orang ketiga). Kata itu adalah simple past tense. Simple past tense nya adalah 'deserved'.

Bentuk spelling kata kerja pada orang ketiga (She, He, It), tergantung dari akhiran kata kerja (verb) tersebut.

1. Untuk kata kerja yang berakhiran -O, -CH, -SH, -X, atau -Z, tambahkan -ES.

Contoh:

go – goes

catch – catches

wash – washes

kiss – kisses

box – boxes

fix – fixes

2. Untuk kata kerja (Verb) yang berakhiran konsonan + Y, hilangkan Y, dan tambahkan -IES.

Contoh:

carry – carries

study – studies

worry – worries

marry – marries

3. Sedangkan, untuk kata kerja yang berakhiran vokal + Y, cukup tambahkan -S.

Contoh:

play – plays

say – says

enjoy – enjoys

A song is defined as a short musical composition of words and music. Lyrics are words that make up a song. We can use songs to practice lots of different language skills. In this case, we can leam English through songs. We would like to learn about how a song is organized (structure) and the social function of songs.

• How to Understand a Song

Song must have meaning. To understand a song, we should know the meaning. The following are ways to figure out a song’s meaning.

A. Listen to the song while reading the lyrics.

B. Read the lyrics on their own to interpret their meaning. You will find the speaker, the word / you, or we refer to.

C. Focus on concrete imagery and sense. Lyrics describe a sense of feeling with images, not just vague ideas or phrases.

D. Unpack or expose unusual or poetic phrases one at time, for example the use of figurative language. They are when the words don't literally mean what they say.
