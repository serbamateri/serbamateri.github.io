2. bagaimana penggunaan kata was dan were


penggunaan was dan were:
was dan were merupakan to be dalam bentuk past/lampau
untuk was : was digunakan untuk subyek I, he, she, it
untuk were : were digunakan untuk subyek they, we, you

contohnya :
was : She was very angry with me yesterday
were : They were surprised two days ago about that news. 
kata was dan were digunakan untuk masa lampau/last.
penggunaan was digunakan untuk subyek I'm,it,she,he
penggunaan were digunakan untuk subyek you,we,they

6. bagaimana dan kapan digunakan kata dari : does & doesn't , were , been , belong , as , did , didn't , won't , take , that , aren't , was , through , ass , would, could , ain't ?


does untuk pertanyaan bentuk simple present (atau penguat suatu pernyataan: she does understand it); doesn't tuk negatif (bentuk simple present), were adalah lampau dari are jadi misal, we were here yesterday; been untuk perfect progressive atau passive, belong adalah verba yang biasa diikuti to bermakna milik  atau bagian dari; as adalah adverb yang biasa dipakai dalam pengerti sebagaimana atau kata sambung yang bermakna sebab dll; did - didn't kata bantu di simple past; won;t negatif dari will tuk future dll; take verba yang memilik ragam makna; that kata tunjuk dan sambung; aren't negatif dari are tuk simple present, was adalah past form dari is tuk simple past; would past form dari will, could past form dari can (would dan could adalah modal untuk memperhalus ungkapan); ain't adalah ungkapan tidak resmi atau slang dalam ragam makna: is not, does not, don't, have/has not  "I ain't got any homework!"

JawabanSimple Past Tense (Verbal/V2)

(+) Marla went to Bali last month.

(-) Marla did not go to Bali last month.

(?) Did Marla go to Bali last month?

Simple Past Tense (Nominal/Was-Were)

(+) Hannah was in my room last night.

(+) They were in John's house last night.

(-) They were not in John's house last night.

(?) Were they in John's house last night?

Penjelasan

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi di waktu lampau (masa lalu). Contohnya: last night, last week, yesterday, dll.

Pola simple past tense verbal (predikat berupa kata kerja):

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object.

(-) Subject + did not + verb 1 + object.

(?) Did + subject + verb 1 + object?

Pola simple past tense nominal/non-verbal (predikat berupa to be):

(+) Subject + was/were + complement.

(-) Subject + was/were not + complement.

(?) Was/were + subject + complement?

I, he, she, it (singular) = gunakan wasThey, we, you (plural) = gunakan were

39. buatlah pertanyaan dari kata HAD,WAS,WERE dan DID masing masing 5 pertanyaan menggunakan bahasa Inggris berserta artinya. ​


Jawaban:

HAD (Telahkah/Pernahkah)

1. Had she visited that museum before? (Apakah dia pernah mengunjungi museum itu sebelumnya?)

2. Had they finished their homework by the time you arrived? (Apakah mereka sudah menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumah mereka saat kamu tiba?)

3. Had he ever traveled to America? (Apakah dia pernah pergi ke Amerika?)

4. Had you met Ilham before he moved to the city? (Apakah kamu pernah bertemu dengan Ilham sebelum dia pindah ke kota?)

WAS (Apakah)

1. Was she at the party last night? (Apakah dia ada di pesta semalam?)

2. Was it raining when you left the house? (Apakah hujan ketika kamu meninggalkan rumah?)

3. Was he feeling sick yesterday? (Apakah dia merasa sakit kemarin?)

4. Was the movie good? (Apakah filmnya bagus?)

5. Was the restaurant crowded when you arrived? (Apakah restorannya ramai saat kamu tiba?)

Were (Apakah (Bentuk Jamak)):

1. Were they at the game yesterday? (Apakah mereka ada di pertandingan kemarin?)

2. Were you and Sarah classmates in college? (Apakah kamu dan Sarah teman sekelas di perguruan tinggi?)

3. Were the children playing in the park this morning? (Apakah anak-anak bermain di taman pagi ini?)

4. Were the students excited about the field trip? (Apakah para siswa senang dengan perjalanan lapangan itu?)

5. Were there any problems with the computer system? (Apakah ada masalah dengan sistem komputer?)

Did (Melakukan/Apa):

1. Did you finish your homework last night? (Apakah kamu menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumahmu semalam?)

2. Did they call you this morning? (Apakah mereka meneleponmu pagi ini?)

3. Did she enjoy the concert? (Apakah dia menikmati konsernya?)

4. Did he eat lunch at the cafeteria? (Apakah dia makan siang di kafetaria?)

5. Did you watch the news on TV yesterday? (Apakah kamu menonton berita di TV kemarin?)

Hope this helps, buat jawaban terverifikasi :)
