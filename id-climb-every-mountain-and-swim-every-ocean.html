15. 1. My sister does her homes work every morning (-) (+) 2 They usally climb the mountain every sunday (-) (+) 3. My Family and I go to the cinema every eveekend (+) (-) tolong Jawab dengan kalimat positif dan negatif​


Simple Present Tense

JAWABAN

1. (+) My sister DOES her homes work every morning.

(-) My sister DOES NOT DO her homes work every morning. (?) DOES My sister DO her homes work every morning.

2. (+) They usually CLIMB the mountain every Sunday.

(-) They DO NOT usually CLIMB the mountain every Sunday. (?) DO they usally CLIMB the mountain every sunday?

3. (+) My Family and I GO to the cinema every weekend.

(-) My Family and I DO NOT GO to the cinema every weekend. (?) DO My Family and I GO to the cinema every weekend.

Catatan!My family and I boleh diganti jadi “we”Do not boleh disingkat jadi “don’t”Does not boleh disingkat jadi “doesn’t”

PEMBAHASAN

Present Tense adalah bentuk kata kerja yang berfungsi untuk menyatakan Kegiatan, rutin, dan kebiasaan. Ciri waktunya:

every morning every sundayevery weekend usually

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C(?) do/does + S + V1 + O/C?

Keterangan:

Jika Subjek He, She, it, satu subjek (my sister)

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb+s/eskalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan DOES

Jika Subjek I, you, we, they, dua subjek (My Family and I)

kalimat positive menyatakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/eskalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan do

25. Change the sentences into the negative and interonegative forms 1. They go to school every day 2. Ririn is washing her dress now 3. We will climb the mountain tommorrow 4. I wrote a letter yesterday 5 we drink milk every morning


Negative and Interrogative Forms:

1. They go to school every day

(-) They do not go to school everyday(?) Do they go to school everyday?

2. Ririn is washing her dress now

(-) Ririn is not washing her dress now(?) Is Ririn washing her dress now?

3. We will climb the mountain tomorrow

(-) We will not climb the mountain tomorrow(?) Will we climb the mountain tomorrow?

4. I wrote a letter yesterday

(-) I did not write a letter yesterday(?) Did I write a letter yesterday?

5. We drink milk every morning

(-) We do not drink milk every morning(?) Do we drink milk every morning?

PEMBAHASANNo. 1 dan 5

Menggunakan Present Tense karena Kegiatan berulang diketahui melalui adanya keterangan waktu “Everyday” dan “Every morning” yang berarti kegiatan tersebut dilakukan secara “setiap hari dan setiap pagi.” Berikut Rumusnya:

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) (-) S + do/does + not + V1 (?) Do/does + S + V1?

No. 2

Menggunakan Present Continuous karena ada keterangan waktu “now” yang berarti Kegiatan tersebut sedang berlangsung sekarang. Berikut Rumusnya:

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing(-) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing(?) is/am/are + S + V-ing?

No. 3

Menggunakan Future Tense karena ada keterangan waktu “tomorrow” yang berarti Kegiatan tersebut akan dilakukan besok. Berikut Rumusnya:

(+) S + will + V1 (-) S + will not + V1 (?) Will + S + V1?

No. 4

Menggunakan Past Tense karena masa lampau diketahui dengan adanya keterangan waktu “yesterday” yang berarti Kegiatan tersebut dilakukan kemarin. Berikut Rumusnya:

(+) S + V2(-) S + did not + V1 (?) Did + S + V1?
