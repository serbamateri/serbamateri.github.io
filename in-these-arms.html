Penjelasan:

Passive voice (kalimat pasif) merupakan jenis kalimat yang memberi penekanan pada aktivitas yang dialami lakukan terhadap objek kalimat. Kalimat pasif adalah lawan dari kalimat aktif yang meletakkan subjek pada awal kalimat guna menekankan aktivitas yang dilakukan oleh subjek.

Struktur Passive Voice

Secara garis besar, struktur kalimat passive voice ialah sebagai berikut.

Object + auxiliary (am, is, are, was, were, be, being, been) + past participle (V3) (+ by + subject (Opsional, bisa ada bisa juga tidak))

Namun penggunaan dalam kalimat tetap harus disesuaikan dengan tenses kalimat. Berikut rumus passive voice untuk tenses dalam Bahasa Inggris.

1. Simple present tense: O + auxiliary (am/ is/ are) + V3

2. Present continuous tense: O + auxiliary (am/ is/ are) + being + V3

3. Present perfect tense: O + have/ has + been + V3

4. Present perfect continuous tense: O + have/ has + been + being + V3

5. Simple past tense: O + auxiliary (was/ were) + V3

6. Past continuous tense: O + auxiliary (was/ were) + being + V3

7. Past perfect tense: O + had + been + V3

8. Past perfect continuous tense: O + had + been + being + V3

9. Simple future tense: O + will + be + V3

10. Future continuous tense: O + will + be + being + V3

11. Future perfect tense: O + will + have + been + V3

12. Future perfect continuous tense: O + will + have + been + being + V3
