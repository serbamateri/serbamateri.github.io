Supaat Mengajar Hitung Integral Tertentu

Perhatikan bahwa

[tex]\begin{aligned}\int_{-1}^3\left(2f(x)-g(x)\right)dx&=\int_{-1}^32f(x)dx-\int_{-1}^3g(x)dx\\&=2\int_{-1}^3f(x)dx-\frac{1}{3}\int_{-1}^33g(x)dx\\&=2\cdot3-\frac{1}{3}\cdot(-6)\\&=6+2\\&=8\end{aligned}[/tex]

₋₁ ∫ ³ [ (2 × f(x)) – g(x) ] dx

= 2 × ₋₁ ∫ ³ [ f(x) ] dx – ½ × ₋₁ ∫ ³ [ 2 × g(x) ] dx
