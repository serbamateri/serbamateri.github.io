4. Dalam suatu tes matematika terdapat 50 soal.perhitungkan nilai tes tersebut menggunakan aturan berikut:jika jawabanya benar,maka nila skornya 4jika jawabanya salah,maka nilai skorya -2jika tidak menjawab maka skornya 0Dalam tes tersebut siti menjawab soal dan memperoleh nilai akhir 144 .berapa jawaban siti yang salah?​


Jawab:

6 soal.

Pembahasan:

Diketahui :

Jumlah soal : 50 buah

Penilaian skor :

Benar (B), skor 4

Salah (S), skor -2

Tidak menjawab (T), skor 0

Soal yg dijawab Siti : 45

Nilai akhir 144

Ditanya :

Berapa soal jawaban Siti yg salah ?

Jawab :

Soal yg dijawab : 45

Perhitungan nilai akhir = 4B - 2S

Persamaan matematikanya :

B + S = 45. (persamaan 1)

4B - 2S = 144. (persamaan 2)

B + S = 45

B = 45 - S. (substitusikan ke dalam persamaan 2)

4B - 2S = 144

4(45 - S) - 2S = 144

180 - 4S - 2S = 144

180 - 6S = 144

-6S = 144 - 180

-6S = -36

S = -36/-6

S = 6

Jadi banyaknya soal jawaban Siti yg salah : 6 soal

Semoga membantu

22. buatlah 5 soal pilihan ganda tentang too and enough beserta kunci jawabanya


Here, I will give brief explanation about “Too and Enough”.

-          TOO is used before adjective.

It is used to say that something is more than we need or want.

In Indonesian TOO means terlalu.

Formula        : too + adjective

Example       :

- The road is too slippery. you will fall.

- These brownies is too sweet. I don’t really like it.

 

-          ENOUGH is used after adjective or before noun.

it is used to say that something is as much as we need or want.

In Indonesia, ENOUGH means cukup.

Formula        : adjective + enough / enough + noun

Example       :

-                      Is your mark good enough this semester?

-                      Do you have enough money to go to Spain?

Answer        :

1.     My homework is ........ difficult. I cannot do it.

a.    Too

b.    Enough

c.    Too many

d.    do not

Answer : A

2.    Does he have ................ time to do all the tasks?

a.    Too

b.    Enough

c.    Too many

d.    many

Answer : B

3.    He is too ...... to drive a motorcycle. He hasn’t got his driving licence yet.

a.    young

b.    old

c.    short

d.    tall

Answer : A

4.    The price of the skirt isn’t too ....... I will buy it.

a.    Big

b.    Small

c.    Short

d.    Expensive

Answer : D

5.    You are ....... enough to reach the top of the shelf. Can you help me to get the red book, please?

a.    Short

b.    Long

c.    tall

d.    Small

Answer : C
