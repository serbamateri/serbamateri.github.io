5. (+) she is an artist (-) (?)


Soal:

(+) she is an artist

(-) (?)

Jawaban:

(-) She is not an artist

(?) is she an artist?

Penjelasan:

Pada kalimat Positive yg tertera adalah kalimat Present Tense bentuk Nominal Sentence. Diketahui melalui:

(+) S + is/am/are + O

= She + is + an artist

(-) S + is/am/are + not + O

= She + is + not + an artist

(?) is/am/are + S + O?

= is + she + an artist?

=== Semoga Membantu ===

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Present Tense

Kode Mapel: 5

13. An artist is a person who


An artist is a person who draws/makes art

Answer:

In English, a definition of artist is not the same as the one in Indonesia. In Indonesia, 'artist' tends to be understood as 'actress, singer, celebrity or simply famous people sometimes. In fact, that is incorrect. An artist is a person who creates works of art as his main job such as sculptor, painter, singer or even chef sometimes but not actress/ actor. They are completely different as they don't create the work of art.

Mapel: English
