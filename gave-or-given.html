23. 1. i gave him my address, and he gave me his. 2. i gave her ............. address, and she gave me.................... 3. he gave me .....................address, and i gave ...................... 4. we gave them ......................address, and they .................... 5. she gave him .....................address, and he gave ................ 6. you gave us ........................address, and we gave .............. 7. they gave you ..................address, and you gave .................


PronounsI gave him my address, and he gave me his.  I gave her my address, and she gave me hers. He gave me his address, and I gave mine.We gave them our address, and they gave theirs.She gave him her address, and he gave hisYou gave us your address, and we gave ours.They gave you their address, and you gave yours.

PEMBAHASAN:

Pronoun merupakan kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan noun (kata benda) yang dapat berupa orang, benda, hewan, tempat, atau konsep abstrak. Kata ganti benda yang merupakan satu dari delapan parts of speechs digunakan untuk menhindari terjadinya pengulangan kata.  

Ada beberapa jenis pronoun. Diantaranya adalah subjective, objective, possessive adjective dan possessive pronoun.  

Perhatikan bentuk subjective pronoun berikut ini!  

I => saya  you => kamu/ kalian  we => kami/ kita  they => mereka  she => dia (perempuan)  he => dia (laki-laki)  it => ini/ itu (hewan/ tumbuhan/ benda)  

Perhatikan bentuk objective pronoun berikut ini!  

me => saya  you => kamu/ kalian  us => kami/ kita  them => mereka  her => dia (perempuan)  him => dia (laki-laki)  it => ini/ itu (hewan/ tumbuhan/ benda)  

Perhatikan bentuk possessive adjective berikut ini!  

my => milik saya  your => milik kamu/ kalian  our => milik kami/ kita  their => milik mereka  her => milik dia (perempuan)  his => milik dia (laki-laki)  its => milik ini/ itu (hewan/ tumbuhan/ benda)  

Perhatikan bentuk possessive pronoun berikut ini!  

mine => milik saya  yours => milik kamu/ kalian  ours => milik kami/ kita  theirs => milik mereka  hers => milik dia (perempuan)  his => milik dia (laki-laki)  its => milik ini/ itu (hewan/ tumbuhan/ benda)

38. she gave me the change after playing some money,change the setence below into passive or active


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: passive voice
Pembahasan:

The sentence 'she gave me the change after paying some money.' is an active sentence.
The passive one would be like this
'I was given the change by her after paying some money.'

39. 1. A standing ovation was ... to the guitarplayer.a. giveb. givingc. gaved. given​


Jawaban :

A standing ovation was given to the guitar player.

Pembahasan :

Kalimat tersebut termasuk kalimat passive yang memiliki pola umum :

Subject + to be + past participle (verb 3)

Pada kalimat tersebut, A standing ovation berperan sebagai subject dan was berperan sebagai to be.

Maka dari itu, kalimat tersebut harus dilengkapi dengan past participle (verb 3) yang terdapat pada pilihan D. given
