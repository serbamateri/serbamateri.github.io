Jawaban:

Here are 10 examples of sentences in both American English and British English:

**American English:**

1. **Color**: The red car is my favorite color.

2. **Favorite**: Pizza is my favorite food.

3. **Apartment**: She lives in a spacious apartment downtown.

4. **Elevator**: Please take the elevator to the third floor.

5. **Soccer**: He enjoys playing soccer with his friends.

6. **Vacation**: We're planning a vacation to the beach next month.

7. **Candy**: She loves eating candy after dinner.

8. **Diaper**: The baby needs a clean diaper.

9. **Cookies**: I baked chocolate chip cookies for the party.

10. **Trunk**: Put the bags in the trunk of the car.

**British English:**

1. **Colour**: The red car is my favourite colour.

2. **Favourite**: Fish and chips is my favourite dish.

3. **Flat**: She lives in a spacious flat in the city centre.

4. **Lift**: Please take the lift to the third floor.

5. **Football**: He enjoys playing football with his mates.

6. **Holiday**: We're planning a holiday to the seaside next month.

7. **Sweets**: She loves eating sweets after tea.

8. **Nappy**: The baby needs a clean nappy.

9. **Biscuits**: I baked chocolate chip biscuits for the party.

10. **Boot**: Put the bags in the boot of the car.

Please note that there are more differences between American English and British English in terms of vocabulary, spelling, and sometimes even grammar. These examples highlight a few common words that differ between the two versions of English.

34. 1.08:15=american: british: 2.09:30=american: british: 3.10:45=american: british: 4.11:55=american: british:​


Jawaban:

1. 08:15= American style: It is eight fifteen.

British style: It is a quarter past eight.

2. 09:30= American style: It is nine thirty.

British style: It is half past nine.

3. 10:45= American style: It is ten forty five.

British style: It is a quarter to eleven.

4. 11:55= American style: It is ten fifty five.

British style:​ It is five minutes to twelve.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Tellig Time (menyatakan waktu) dengan British Style dan American Style dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Jika jarum panjang berada di sebelah kanan jam dinding (kurang dari 30 menit) maka menggunakan "past" yang berarti melewati pukul.

Jika jarum panjang berada di sebelah kiri (lebih dari 30 menit) maka menggunakan "to" yang berarti menuju pukul.  

Jika jarum panjang persis di menit ke 30, maka boleh memilih untuk menggunakan "past" atau "to".
