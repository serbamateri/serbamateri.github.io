19. Making passive voice: 1. Plants need water in order to survive 2. Forests produce vital oxygen 3. Tornadoes break tree branches and bend trees 4. Smog can irritate the eyes and throat 5. Flood can wipe away farms, trees, and many more heavy items 6. Gases can build up, and create outward pressure 7. Volcanic eruptions often cause temporary food shortages 8. Asphalt and concrete store heat longer and gradually release heat at night 9. Malnutrition can cause serious physical and mental health problems 10. Humans destroy animal habitats in a number of different ways such as clearing forests, draining rivers to bring water to those crops, and paving over meadows


PART : ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE IN AN ARTICLE

------------------------

Jawaban:

1. Water is needed (by plants) in order to survive.

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+Verb 3 +by + Agent   ( P.V of Simple present )

Subject ( object in the active voice )

Please remember, we must change the verb "need" into the past participle form "needed."

2. Vital oxygen is produced (by forests )

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb 3 +by + Agent ( P.V of simple present )

Please remember subject "Vital oxygen" is singular . We can't count it .

We must change the verb "produce" into the past participle form "produced."

3.Tree branches and bend trees are broken (by tornadoes)

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb 3 +by + Agent (P.V of simple present )

Keep in mind, we must pay attention to the subject form whether singular or plural.

4. The eyes and throat can be irritated ( by smog )

Pattern : S+can+be+verb 3 + by + Agent ( P.V of modal auxiliary )

5. Farms, trees, and many more heavy items can be wiped away (by flood)

Pattern : S+can+be+verb 3 + by + Agent ( P.V of modal auxiliary )

6. Outward pressure can be created and it is built up (by gasses )

or Gasses can build up and outward pressure can be created (by them)

Pattern : S+can+be+verb 3 + by + Agent ( P.V of modal auxiliary )

Outward pressure is the effect of building gasses.

Please pay attention to the context of the sentence to make it clear .

7. Temporary food shortages are often caused (by volcanic eruptions)

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+Adv.frequency +verb 3 +by + Agent (  P.V of simple present  especially in habitual activity )

Food shortages is plural, because it can be counted and it is more often to happen.

8. Heat is stored (by asphalt and concrete) longer and gradually released at night.

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb 3 +by + Agent (P.V of simple present )

Keep in mind, we must adjust the context of the sentence to make it clear.

9. Physical and mental health problems can be caused (by malnutrition )

Pattern : S+can+be+verb 3 + by + Agent ( P.V of modal auxiliary )

10.  Animal habitats are destroyed (by humans ) in a number of different ways such as clearing forests , draining rivers to bring water to those crops, and paving over meadows.

Pattern : S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb 3 +by + Agent ( P.V of simple present )

Penjelasan:

PASSIVE VOICEDalam penulisan formal, Passive voice menekankan pada tindakan dan obyek yang dikenakan tindakan daripada mengekspose si pembuat perkara. Harap diperhatikan baik-baik, bahwa tidak semua kata kerja yang terlampir dalam kalimat aktif bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive. Passive voice tidak bisa merubah kata kerja tanpa obyek ( Intransitive verb ).  Contoh : Go, sleep, occur, happen and etc.Perubahan kalimat passive , menyesuaikan tenses pada masing-masing kalimat aktif. Ciri khas kalimat pasif , menggunakan BE + verb 3 (past participle )Jika ingin merubah kalimat Aktif ke dalam bentuk Passive Voice, tentukan dahulu subyek dalam kalimat aktif tersebut. Subyek ini akan di posisikan sebagai Agent dalam kalimat pasif. Penerapan By+Agent dapat dihilangkan karena tidak dianggap penting.Selanjutnya, tentukan obyek dalam kalimat aktif . Obyek kalimat aktif akan diposisikan sebagai Subyek dalam kalimat pasif.Harap diperhatikan pronoun nya ( kata ganti orang ). Pronoun akan berubah berdasarkan posisi kelas kata ( parts of speech ) dalam kalimat pasif.Ciri khas kalimat aktif , menggunakan bentuk kata kerja awalan Me-.............,Sedangkan kalimat pasif , menggunakan bentuk kata kerja awalan Di-..............
