13. Samuel lost 9 kg after joining a gym. This is 12 % of previous mass. What is Samuel's mass now


Jawaban:

66 kg

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]m_p[/tex] = Samuel's previous mass

[tex]m_n[/tex] = Samuel's mass now

[tex]\frac{12}{100}m_p=9[/tex]

[tex]m_p= 9×100÷12=75[/tex]

[tex]m_n =m_p-9=75-9=66[/tex] kg
